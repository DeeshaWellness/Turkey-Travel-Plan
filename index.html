Additional Resources

Local Transportation

  • Istanbul Public Transport: Istanbul’s extensive network includes buses, metros, ferries, and trams. Istanbulkart is a rechargeable card that offers a convenient way to pay for public transport.

  • Cappadocia Transportation: For navigating Cappadocia’s unique landscape, local minibuses (dolmuş) and car rentals are popular options. Information on routes and schedules can usually be found at hotel receptions or local tourist offices.

  • Antalya Transport: Antalya’s public transport system consists of buses and trams. The Antalyakart is the city’s transport card.

  • Turkish Culture and Tourism Office: For a comprehensive guide to Turkey’s vast cultural and natural offerings, visit the official tourism website.

  • Lonely Planet’s Guide to Turkey: Offers in-depth travel advice and local tips. Check out Lonely Planet Turkey for more insights.
